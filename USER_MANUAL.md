## Logging In
To access the administrative panel:
1. Navigate to `/login` on your website.
2. Enter your **Operator ID** (Email) and **Security Key** (Password).
3. Click **Establish Connection**.

## The Command Center
The Dashboard (Command Center) provides a bird's-eye view of your organization:
- **Stats:** Track visitors, active cases, and new evidence.
- **Recent Logs:** See who edited what and when.
- **Quick Actions:** Buttons to immediately start a new investigation or upload media.

## Managing Investigations
The **Investigations** section is where Case Files are stored.
- **Create New:** Click the "New Investigation" button.
- **Status:** 
    - *Draft:* Only visible to you.
    - *Published:* Visible to the public.
    - *Archived:* Hidden from the main archive but kept for records.

## Using the Visual Editor
Our "Haunting Editor" (TipTap) allows you to create immersive pages without code:
- **Headings:** Use H1 for titles and H2 for sections.
- **Rich Text:** Bold, Italics, and Blockquotes for dramatic witness testimony.
- **Evidence:** Click the Image icon to embed photos or the Video icon for YouTube links.
- **Autosave:** Your progress is saved as you type.

## Handling Community Stories
Visitors can submit their own paranormal encounters.
1. Go to **Community Stories**.
2. Review the submission.
3. Click **Verify** to publish it to the community portal.

## Media Management
The **Media Library** holds all your evidence (Photos, Audio, Video).
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, MP3, MP4.
- Always use high-contrast images for the cinematic look.

## Site Settings
Change the organization name, contact email, and donation links from the **Settings** menu.
